How it works?

1. Just choose any string as your shared key and share it with your friend(s).
2. Enter the string in the text box below and click "Go!".
3. That's it! Anything you and your friends edit in the notepad is now synchronized at lightning fast speed!
4. After you're done collaborating and sharing your ideas, you can save and download your notepad contents as a text file. Later you can open that file or any other text file to start editing!
